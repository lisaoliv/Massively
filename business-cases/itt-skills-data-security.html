ITT Skills LLC
Data Security & Salesforce Intake

Business case for safeguarding regulated personal data and modernizing the student intake process with Salesforce.

Context

ITT Skills LLC is an IT training provider that collected regulated personal identifiers (PII) on paper forms and unprotected spreadsheets. The company needs to comply with Massachusetts data security regulations while keeping intake simple for applicants and staff.

Objectives

  • Protect PII with strong authentication, encryption, and least-privilege access
  • Standardize intake data so applicant status and eligibility are clear at every step
  • Create auditable records for approvals, document uploads, and support interactions
  • Reduce manual handling of sensitive data in email and shared drives

Proposed Salesforce intake flow

  • Applicants authenticate to a branded Experience Cloud site with MFA
  • Guided intake form captures only required PII with client-side validation
  • Files (IDs, eligibility proof) upload to encrypted storage with virus scanning
  • Automated routing creates a Case and tasks for admissions and finance
  • Approvals log decisions; Shield Event Monitoring tracks access and downloads
  • Notifications keep applicants informed without exposing sensitive fields in email

Security controls

  • MFA and SSO-ready authentication for staff and portal users
  • TLS in transit; platform encryption and field-level encryption for PII at rest
  • Role-based access via profiles and permission sets; restricted report types
  • Data classification and DLP rules for Social Security Numbers and IDs
  • Audit trails for field history, approvals, and file access
  • Backups, disaster recovery plan, and quarterly access reviews

Deliverables

  • Current-state risk assessment of intake and storage practices
  • Future-state Salesforce intake blueprint with swimlanes and data model
  • Security and compliance checklist mapped to MA 201 CMR 17.00
  • Implementation roadmap with phases, owners, and success metrics
